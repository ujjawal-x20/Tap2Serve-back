Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up.

How long does implementation take?

expand_more
Typically, we can get your menu digitized and hardware shipped within 7-10 business days. Our onboarding team handles the heavy lifting, including menu entry and staff training.

Can I use my own iPad?

expand_more
Yes! Tap2Serve is compatible with most modern iOS and Android tablets. However, for the best reliability and the "Offline Mode" feature, we recommend our dedicated hardware suite.

Is there a contract?

expand_more
Our Starter and Professional plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Enterprise plans typically involve an annual agreement to unlock custom development and lower processing rates.

What happens if my internet goes down?

expand_more
Tap2Serve includes a robust "Offline Mode". Your POS terminals will continue to process orders and print tickets. Data syncs automatically once connectivity is restored, ensuring zero downtime.

Can I manage multiple locations?

expand_more
Absolutely. Our Enterprise dashboard allows you to toggle between locations, push menu updates to all venues simultaneously, and view aggregated sales reports from a single login.

Do you integrate with delivery apps?

expand_more
Yes, we have direct integrations with major delivery platforms like UberEats, DoorDash, and Zomato. Orders from these platforms appear directly on your KDS, eliminating double entry.

Is payment processing secure?

expand_more
We use PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant processing for all transactions. Customer card data is end-to-end encrypted and never stored on your local devices, ensuring maximum security.
